Moons Singing

Published on June 3rd

You compliment Moons singing and he gets flustered about it...

You were sitting at the security desk in the daycare, it was nap time so you let handle the kids since you couldn't see too well because of how dark it was. You were scrolling through social media when you heard singing coming from the general direction of the nap time corner. The singing sounded very pretty and soothing, and it sounded like some kind of lullaby. After a few minutes, Moon walked over, "Was that you singing?" you asked, already knowing the answer. He froze, red eyes widening as he blushed, "I-I-um-N-No" He stuttered. "Who else would it be? There's no way one of the kids could sing that good! Your singing sounds amazing!" you whisper-yelled, he pulled his hat down, covering part of his blushing face "T-thank you, Starlight"
